This server downloads books from https://www.fimfiction.net and converts them before passing them to you. The following two urls download The Greatest Equine Who has Ever Lived! in epub or mobi format:

  • /book/9/download/book.epub
  • /book/9/download/book.mobi

This server performs two operations. It embeds all external images in the ebook and converts it to epub and mobi using calibre (this ensure a consistent and error-free ebook).
